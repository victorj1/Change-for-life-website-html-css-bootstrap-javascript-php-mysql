Corporate Wellness Programs: How to Create Job Love Within Your Organization


Happy Employees Are Productive Employees

Our corporate workshops are designed to create an emotionally intelligent, productive and motivated workforce and leadership. Having worked in the corporate culture and leadership for many years, we have first-hand experience on its unique challenges. We are a big advocate on making the workplace a safe, inclusive and productive environment. With our unique knowledge of the neuroscience of change, the psychology of achievement, productivity, success and work-life balance, we are confident that we can create positive changes in your company culture.


In our sessions, we take you through restorative body movement postures that release tension, build strength and flexibility, increasing your mobility and range of motion. We educate you on how to practice alignment in your everyday life to improve your physical health and wellbeing. We use a blend of knowledge of alignment-based yoga, pilates, pain management and restorative movement to create an accessible and educational experience personalized for you.
